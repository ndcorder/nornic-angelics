The Index of Unfinished Sentences

Compiled from the papers of Eleanor Voss (1962–2019)

with Addendum

Being a Complete Catalogue of Sentences Interrupted by Circumstance, Omission, or Design, Arranged Alphabetically and by Category, with Page References to the Unpublished Manuscript

The manuscript to which this index refers was found among the compiler's personal effects following her death in November 2019. Despite extensive efforts by the estate, no complete version of the text has been recovered. What remains is this index — the only portion Mrs. Voss had finished preparing — and a few scattered marginal notes reproduced herein. The page numbers, where legible, have been preserved exactly as written. Where the compiler's handwritten annotations appear, they are enclosed in [square brackets]. Otherwise, the index speaks for itself. — The Estate of Eleanor Voss, 2021

A note on the page numbers: the surviving manuscript fragments suggest a total length of approximately 310 pages in standard typescript. The highest page number referenced in this index is 301.
