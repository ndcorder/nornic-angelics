## Critic Review

Operating Instructions for a Body You're Leaving is the portfolio's poetry peak and one of its most devastating artifacts — a decommissioning manual for vacating a body, rendered in such perfect technical-register calm that every procedural instruction reads as eulogy and every appendix reads as confession. The specificity is extraordinary and never decorative: the sediment layer in the grief reservoir composed of "accumulated instances of tenderness that settled because you failed to circulate them when given the chance," the backs of the knees as the repository for "sudden trust in strangers," the three rare intact capacities ("the ability to fall asleep in the presence of another") that should be kept because "they will not be found in better condition elsewhere." The surprise is distributed and structural — the scar classification system that reads as cartography, Section 4's "there is no appendix for it" dropping into silence after four pages of meticulous procedure, Appendix B's "spot behind the left ear" discovered too late — and the revision history is a masterwork of recursive worldbuilding: v4.0 "clutched in the hands of a body that had completed the procedure. The pages were damaged beyond recovery." This pairs with Termination for Convenience and The Aesthetics Committee as the portfolio's triptych on bureaucratic erasure, but where those forged government and civic apparatus, this one forges something more intimate — a manual for the bureaucracy of leaving yourself. The domain that has been the portfolio's weakest since iteration 1 has produced its most accomplished work.
