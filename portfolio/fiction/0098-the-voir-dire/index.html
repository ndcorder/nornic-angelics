The Voir Dire

You have been summoned for jury duty in a matter pending before this court.

The following questionnaire is administered to determine your suitability to serve as an impartial juror. Your answers will become part of the official record.

Please answer each question truthfully. There are no correct responses.

Question I

What is your full legal name?

Question II

Have you ever served on a jury before?

Question III

Do you have a personal or professional relationship with any of the parties in this matter?

Question IV

Is there any reason you believe you cannot render a fair and impartial verdict?

Question V

Have you ever been called to account for something you did — or didn't do — and felt the judgment was unfair?

Question VI

Is there someone you think about more than they think about you?

Question VII

When was the last time you kept something that wasn't yours to keep?
