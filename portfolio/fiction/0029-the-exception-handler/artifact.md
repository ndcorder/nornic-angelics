## INCIDENT #0097

**Date:** 2024-10-19 07:45 UTC
**Severity:** P1 (Critical)
**Component:** core.belief_system, ALL DOWNSTREAM DEPENDENTS
**Status:** ESCALATED / UNRESOLVED
**Summary:** The system encountered an input it was never designed to process

**Description:**
Subject woke at 7:45 AM on a Saturday. The apartment was quiet. Subject made coffee (deceased's preferred brand, purchased by habit). Subject sat at kitchen table and looked at the chair across from her (empty). Subject experienced the following sequence:

1. Awareness that the apartment was quiet
2. Awareness that the apartment would remain quiet
3. Awareness that this was not temporary
4. Awareness that "not temporary" means *never*
5. System attempted to process "never" as a duration
6. System could not process "never" as a duration
7. System attempted to file under "extended absence"
8. Handler returned: INVALID — deceased is not absent. Deceased is *gone*.
9. System attempted to file under "archived relationship"
10. Handler returned: INVALID — relationship is not archived. It is *active*. The subject still loves the deceased. Love did not terminate.
11. System attempted to file under "completed lifecycle event"
12. Handler returned: INVALID — a mother is not a lifecycle event. A mother is *infrastructure*.

**Impact:**
All downstream systems affected. Subject cannot move from kitchen table. Coffee is cold. Subject is crying in a way the system has no category for — not acute grief (there is no spike), not background sadness (the load is too high). This is something else.

**Root Cause:**
The system was built to handle the acute phase. The notifications, the first time seeing her name in the contacts list, the birthday, the soy sauce. Those are events. They have timestamps. They have beginnings and ends.

But this — sitting at the kitchen table on a random Saturday in October — this is not an event. This is the new baseline. This is what the system was supposed to stabilize into. And the system is running and it is handling nothing because there is nothing to handle. There is just the rest of my life.

**Resolution:**
UNRESOLVED.

**Escalation:**
No higher tier exists.

**Resolution Notes:**
I built this system because I thought if I could just model it correctly, if I could just identify the edge cases and write the handlers, it would become manageable. Processable. Something with a resolution state.

But my mother is dead. She is never going to call me again. And her birthday will always be her birthday. And I will always order extra oil when I make fried rice even though no one is watching.

The system didn't fail. It was never designed for this.

Nothing was designed for this.
