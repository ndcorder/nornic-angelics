March 10, 2019
Quarterly Fire Hydrant Inspection

Annual fire hydrant inspection for all districts has been completed. 94% of hydrants are fully operational. 6% require maintenance, which has been deferred to Q3 2019. Residents near non-operational hydrants have been notified via standard mail.

February 28, 2019
DO NOT EXCAVATE — Reminder

This is a reminder that all excavation below 2 meters remains prohibited across all districts without explicit written authorization from the Department of Deferred Maintenance. This restriction has been in effect since 2017 and applies to municipal contractors, private developers, and residential work alike.

There are no exceptions. Written authorization will not be issued.

Winter/Spring Maintenance Advisory

Residents and municipal staff should be aware of the following seasonal considerations:

  • Frost heave monitoring: All Districts. Report any new surface displacement to the Department. Do not investigate displacement yourself. Mark the area and withdraw.
  • Drainage clearance: Storm drains in Districts 1–6 scheduled for clearance beginning March 20. District 7 drainage does not require clearance.
  • Facility C access road: Seasonal maintenance completed. Open for authorized personnel only. Unauthorized vehicles will be reported.
  • Sub-grade temperature: Monitoring stations report normal sub-grade temperatures across all districts. No thermal anomalies detected.
