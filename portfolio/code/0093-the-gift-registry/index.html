The Registry Is Complete

Clara wrote
"I think love is the thing you can't stop believing in, even when you should. Even when believing is the thing that's keeping you from leaving."
James wrote
"I think love is the thing you can't stop running from, even when you want to stay. Even when staying is the only thing you were ever any good at."
They each bought the same cutting board, three weeks apart, from the same store. Neither mentioned it. Clara's is still in the trunk of her car. James's is in his office, in the bag. They registered for a third one — together — because it was easier than asking why they each needed their own.

They've been preparing for the same ending. They've been doing it alone. They will probably continue.
Some gifts choose themselves.
